Prince Louis Rwagasore

1932-1962

Biography

Prince Louis Rwagasore, born in 1932, was an iconic Burundian leader and a staunch advocate for his country's independence. As the founder of the Union for National Progress (UPRONA), he played a significant role in the fight against colonization and the unification of Burundi. Unfortunately, his life was tragically cut short in 1961 when he was assassinated just days before the elections that could have made him the Prime Minister of an independent Burundi. His legacy continues to inspire future generations to strive for freedom, justice, and national unity.
